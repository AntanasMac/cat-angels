Cat angels descend upon earth. Bringing blessings , guidance, and love to those in need of it, they fly on the tailwind of love, always there to hug the needy. The best ones are those with incredible beauty, exotic colors, wing spans that reach well beyond my car. Others with spotted feathers, an emerald green eye, and a warm tone that will melt a heart. When the cloud clears and a ray of sunshine finds you, you are in love with the light and delicate angel.
